
## 工作原理

A DC motor operates based on the principle of the **Lorentz force**, which states that a current-carrying wire placed in a magnetic field experiences a physical force. When direct current flows through the coil inside the motor, the interaction with the permanent magnets creates a torque that spins the rotor, while a device called a **commutator** automatically reverses the current direction every half-turn to ensure the motor keeps rotating continuously in the same direction.

##### 中文翻译
 直流电机的工作原理基于**洛伦兹力**（通电导体在磁场中受力）的定律。当直流电通过电机内部的线圈时，电流与磁场的相互作用产生转矩推动转子旋转；同时，依靠一个叫做**换向器**的装置在旋转过程中自动改变线圈中的电流方向，从而确保电机能够持续不断地朝同一个方向旋转。
