## 工作原理

A **transformer** operates based on electromagnetic induction. When **alternating current** flows through the primary coil, it generates **a changing magnetic field** within the **iron core**; this fluctuating field then passes through the secondary coil and induces a new voltage in it, allowing electrical energy to be transferred and voltage levels to be converted without any direct electrical contact between the two coils.

##### 中文翻译
**变压器**的工作原理基于电磁感应：当**交流电**通过初级线圈时，会在**铁芯**中产生**不断变化的**磁场，这个变化的磁场穿过次级线圈并在其中感应出新的电压，从而在两个线圈没有直接电接触的情况下，实现了电能的传输和电压等级的转换。


#### 包含过程版本

(1) 电生磁

When an alternating voltage (AC Source) is applied to the primary winding, it forces an alternating current to flow through the coil. According to Ampere's Law, this flowing current generates a magnetic field around the coil. Because the current is constantly changing direction and magnitude (AC), the magnetic field it creates is also time-varying (constantly expanding and collapsing).

中文：

当交流电压施加到一次侧绕组时，它驱动交流电流流过线圈。根据安培定律，流动的电流会在线圈周围产生磁场。因为电流的大小和方向在不断变化，所以它产生的磁场也是随时间变化的（不断地膨胀和收缩）。

（2）一次侧如何传递到二次侧

The iron core  has high permeability, meaning it traps the changing magnetic flux generated by the primary side and guides it to flow through the secondary winding. When this changing magnetic flux passes through the secondary coil, Faraday's Law of Induction takes effect: a changing magnetic field inside a coil induces a voltage across its terminals. Thus, energy is transferred wirelessly via the magnetic field.

铁芯具有高导磁率，这意味着它能捕获一次侧产生的变化磁通量，并将其引导穿过二次侧绕组。当这个变化的磁通量穿过二次侧线圈时，法拉第电磁感应定律发生作用：线圈内变化的磁场会在其端子上感应出电压。因此，能量通过磁场被“无线”传递了过去。
