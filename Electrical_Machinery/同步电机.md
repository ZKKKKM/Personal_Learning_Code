
## 基本原理

A synchronous motor operates when **AC power** in the **stator** generates a **Rotating Magnetic Field (RMF)** at a _constant speed_. Meanwhile, the **rotor** is fed with **DC supply** to become a strong electromagnet with _fixed poles_. Due to the magnetic attraction, the rotor poles experience **magnetic locking** with the stator field, pulling the rotor to spin at the exact **synchronous speed**. Unlike induction motors, there is **no slip**, meaning the rotor rotates at the _same rate_ as the magnetic field frequency.


#### 中文版本

同步电机的工作原理是：**定子**通入**交流电**产生以_恒定速度_移动的**旋转磁场**；同时，**转子**通过**直流电源**励磁成为具有_固定磁极_的电磁铁。由于磁力吸引，转子磁极与定子磁场发生**磁力互锁**，牵引转子以精确的**同步转速**旋转。与感应电机不同，同步电机**不存在转差**，这意味着转子转速与磁场频率保持_相同的速率_。
