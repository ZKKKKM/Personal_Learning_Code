## 工作原理

An induction motor works because **AC power** in the **stator** creates a **Rotating Magnetic Field (RMF)** moving at a _constant speed_. This field cuts the **rotor conductors**, creating an **induced current** through _electromagnetic induction_. The interaction between this current and the magnetic field generates **Lorentz force**, producing **torque** that turns the rotor in the _same direction_. The rotor always spins _slightly slower_ than the field, which is called **slip**, to keep generating force.


###### 中文翻译

感应电机的工作原理是：当**交流电**通过**定子**时，产生一个以_恒定速度_旋转的**旋转磁场**；该磁场切割**转子导体**，通过_电磁感应_产生**感应电流**。该电流与磁场相互作用产生**洛伦兹力**，从而形成**转矩**驱动转子朝_相同方向_旋转；转子转速始终_稍慢于_磁场转速（即存在**转差**），以维持感应和力的产生。
